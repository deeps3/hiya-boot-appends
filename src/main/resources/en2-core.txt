I am so sorry for any inconvenience caused by me. Hope you can accept my apology and arrange a new time for me to visit you. I am looking forward to your reply.
Yours sincerely,
Li Ming

（1）选择餐厅时关注因素
Emerging from the clearly depicted pie chart is the distribution of focusing factors of citizens in a city when choosing a restaurant in 2017, consisting of 4 parts, which are features, service, environment, price and other factors. Among them, the proportion of service, environment, price and other factors is 26.8%, 23.8%, 8.4% and 4.7% respectively. By contrast, the factor of focusing on features of the restaurant is in the lead, accounting for 36.3%.从清晰描绘的饼状图中可以看出2017年城市居民在选择餐厅时关注因素的分布情况，由4个部分组成，分别是特色、服务、环境、价格等因素。其中，服务、环境、价格等因素所占比例分别为26.8%、23.8%、8.4%和4.7%。相比之下，注重餐厅特色的因素占主导地位，占36.3%。

What has triggered this phenomenon? To begin with, with the fast development of national economy and personal wealth, people in China have stepped into an era of enjoying life, transforming traditional pattern of living. Therefore, such a great proportion of citizens are more likely to focus on the features of a restaurant, instead of only concentrating on the price. Moreover, in a society where living standard is highly advocated, citizens in mounting numbers in China, shrugging off their former habit of focusing on lower price, gradually find the service and environment of a restaurant is an essential factor. According to a survey conducted by China Research Center, up to 87% Chinese people prefer to choose a comfortable and fashionable restaurant when they go out for eating.是什么引发了这种现象?首先，随着国民经济和个人财富的快速发展，中国人民已经步入了一个享受生活的时代，传统的生活方式正在发生转变。因此，如此大比例的市民更有可能关注一家餐厅的特色，而不是仅仅关注价格。此外，在一个崇尚生活水平的社会中，越来越多的中国公民摆脱了以往注重低价的习惯，逐渐发现餐馆的服务和环境是必不可少的因素。根据中国研究中心的一项调查，多达87%的中国人在外出就餐时更喜欢选择舒适、时尚的餐厅。

Taking into account what has been argued so far, I am inclined to think about the current situation is normal. In view of the analysis above, it can be predicted that the trend will continue in the future. Accordingly, it is of no necessity for the public to regard it with too much consideration. 考虑到目前为止的争论，我倾向于认为目前的情况是正常的。根据以上分析，可以预测，这种趋势在未来还会继续。因此，公众没有必要对它考虑太多。



（2）毕业去向分析
The graph shows that changes in the choices of graduates of a certain university in fives time from 2013 to 2018. According to the bar charts, a significant portion of students sought employment after graduation, this portion accounts 68% in 2013 and dropped slightly to 60% in 2018. And a modest portion of graduates wanted to pursue further education, with the number grew sightly from 26% to 34% in five years time. Of the three choices, setting up a company was obviously the minority’s choice. as it is shown, only 1.3% choose to start a business and the figure rose to 2.6% at the end of the period..图表显示了从2013年到2018年某所大学毕业生在五次选择上的变化。根据柱状图，很大一部分学生毕业后找工作，这部分在2013年占68%，在2018年略有下降到60%。少数毕业生希望继续深造，这一比例在五年内从26%大幅上升至34%。在三个选择中，成立公司显然是少数人的选择。如图所示，只有1.3%的人选择创业，这个数字在期末上升到了2.6%。
The reasons behind might be as follows. Most students would prefer to find a job so that they could gain the skills and experience that are crucial for a brighter career path; Besides, most jobs only require that applicants have bachelor degree so most graduates are qualified. Despite of that, the competition for well-paid jobs is still fierce, accompanied with universities’ policy to expand the number of postgraduates,  more students choose to pursue higher degree. At last, it is very challenging to start a businiess for graduates who lack the resources and experience, so that number was very small. With more favorable conditions provided by government to encourage high-technology start-ups, some graduates were willing to take the opportunity to become entrepreneurs.背后的原因可能如下。大多数学生更愿意找一份工作，这样他们就可以获得技能和经验，这对光明的职业道路是至关重要的;此外，大多数工作只要求申请人有学士学位，所以大多数毕业生都是合格的。尽管如此，高薪工作的竞争依然激烈，伴随着大学扩大研究生数量的政策，更多的学生选择追求更高的学位。最后，对于缺乏资源和经验的毕业生来说，创业是非常具有挑战性的，所以这个数字非常小。由于政府为鼓励高科技创业提供了更有利的条件，一些毕业生愿意抓住机会成为企业家。
Overall, from 2013 to 2018, majority of students found employment after graduation and the number fell slightly. A smaller number choose to pursue higher degree, and the smallest number choose to start a business, though the last two groups saw a light growth总体而言，从2013年到2018年，大多数学生毕业后找到了工作，人数略有下降。更少的人选择攻读更高的学位，最小的人选择创业，尽管后两类人的增长幅度不大。
